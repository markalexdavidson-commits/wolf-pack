🐺 Your Project Overview

You are creating a fitness concept + app idea called:

THE PACK

A community-based fitness platform inspired by Wolf Pack Fitness, but with a more flexible, accessible model.

⸻

💡 Core Idea

Instead of only offering memberships, your idea is:

A free Progressive Web App (PWA) that allows anyone to join workouts on a pay-per-session basis.

⸻

📱 How the App Works

* Users download The Pack (free)
* They receive notifications about upcoming workouts
* They can browse sessions (time, location, price)
* They can join and pay per session

👉 No membership required

⸻

🎯 Target Audience

* Casual athletes
* Busy students
* People who can’t commit to memberships
* Anyone who wants to stay connected to the community

⸻

🔥 Unique Selling Point

Flexibility + community

Unlike traditional gyms:

* ❌ No long-term commitment
* ✅ Pay only when you train
* ✅ Still part of “The Pack”

⸻

🧠 Key Term You Learned

PWA (Progressive Web App)

👉 A website that works like an app
👉 Much cheaper than building a real app

⸻

💰 Cost Breakdown

* Real app: 30,000–100,000+ AED
* Your PWA: ~2,000–6,000 AED

👉 Fits your 10,000 AED budget

⸻
